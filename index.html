About

I am an Assistant Professor (RTDb) at the Department of Information Engineering of the University of Padova.
My research interests include information access and retrieval, topic modeling, and word embedding techniques, in particular

  • Information Retrieval Models
  • Methodologies to support expert users in multidisciplinary areas
  • Modeling and experimenting with the usage of themes/topics
  • Word embedding in longitudinal corpora
